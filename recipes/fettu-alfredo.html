Fettuccine Alfredo

Description:

A recipe of a Fettuccine Alfredo to die for.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Melt the butter and cream together on the stove, season, and stir in the cheese.
  3. Toss the pasta in the cheese sauce.
